My Journey

From Passion to Profession

I discovered my love for coding in 9th grade when I first encountered HTML in my computer science book. The elegance of writing code captivated me. By 10th grade, I was learning C language, which fueled my motivation to dive deeper into programming.

Sharing my passion with my brother, who was studying for a BS in Computer Science at UET Peshawar, proved to be a turning point. He guided me on a roadmap to transform my passion into a profession. Starting with HTML, which I had already mastered, he encouraged me to learn CSS and build projects that would solidify my understanding.

As I progressed, I explored the Bootstrap framework, creating various templates that honed my design skills. Once I felt comfortable in the design phase, my brother advised me to tackle JavaScript. It was challenging at first, but persistence paid off, and I soon became adept at it.

Next came backend development, where I learned PHP for server-side programming and MySQL for database management. I dedicated over a year and a half to mastering Laravel, a PHP framework that allowed me to develop professional applications. Recently, I’ve embraced Livewire to enhance user experience.

With a solid backend foundation, I shifted my focus to frontend development, beginning with React.js and later mastering Next.js, a powerful framework for building dynamic applications.

My journey is a combination of three key areas:

  • Web Designing: Learning HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript
  • Backend Development: Mastering PHP, MySQL, Laravel, and Livewire
  • Frontend Development: Gaining expertise in React.js and Next.js
